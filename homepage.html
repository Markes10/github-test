City Management Platforms

A Unified Platform that supports the overall management and
control of the city and cross-department collaboration. It provides
full stack AI capabilities which utilize the current infrastructure,
unify standalone systems, and upgrade the intelligence level of
current systems.

Vehicle Access Management

The tool enables intelligent automation of car parking spaces and
access control. Applications include automatic number plate
recognition, under-vehicle scanning, space and vehicle flow
optimization, abandoned vehicle detection, vehicle count, time
and live location tracking, mapping vehicle/driver identities to a
database, and integrations with multiple automation devices and
various CRMs.

AI Video Analytics

Applications include crowd control, loitering, overcrowding, line
queue counting, restricted area access, and wrong-way detection.
The system can object-match, distinguish abandoned or removed
objects, alert camera sabotage and tamper, scan restricted areas
for intrusions. Face detection technology assists with facemask
controls, people-tracking, injured individuals, and recognizing
different emotions.

Vehicle Smart Checkpoints

This service provides field security guards with integrated decision
support tools for access management. The system automates the
flow of vehicle and people identification, correlation with
inspections for security, accreditation, and logistics scheduling.
